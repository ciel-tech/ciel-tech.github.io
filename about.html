Ciel Technologies was formed in 2009 and in this time has become a company that is rapidly growing in reputation for providing a cost effective and quality service.

We enjoy high success rates with data recovery and this is attributed to the continued investment we make in new recovery techniques when they become available and the constant training we subject ourselves to so we are fully prepared for any repair eventuality. Self improvement is the key to this company becoming so successful in the data recovery field. Along with the skills commitment we also pride ourselves on our customer service.

We aim to provide an individualistic approach when dealing with our customers so that we make them feel they are being looked after and that the data recovery process is explained at every step. Ciel Technologies Data Recovery continues to provide service for both domestic customers and business users alike. We afford the same high standard of service to all our clients regardless of the type of storage media, cost or the time taken to complete the work.

 

DATA RECOVERY SKILLS

 

Ciel Technologies Data Recovery specialize in Hard Disk Drives, Raid Array and NAS,  Solid State Drives, USB and Memory Cards, Software Systems, Mobile Phones and Tablets and CD, DVD, Floppy and Tape. Our NAND reading techniques put us in a very competitive position and we are often successful even where other companies have failed. We can deal with most failed hard drive symptoms ranging from electronic problems to physical mechanical problems. All mechanical hard drive issues we work with are dealt within an EU class clean room means that these repairs can be carried out in a safe, dust free environment.

 

DATA ERASURE AND DESTRUCTION

 

Ciel Technologies Data Recovery also offers free data erasure and hard drive disposal services, this ensures that your data will never fall into the wrong hands once you have no more use for your hard drive. Our clients come from all over Kampala and East Africa. We also have dealt with clients in Europe.
